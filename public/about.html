Hi, I'm Paridhi — a creative technologist and experiential designer based in New York. I graduated with a Bachelor's in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design. My creative practice revolves around designing and developing experimental web interfaces and interactions, as well as sensor-based physical immersive experiences, with the intent of bringing people together and introducing creative ways of thinking.

Through this work, I aim to encourage people to engage with their physical surroundings, using technology to deepen their connection to the physical world rather than distancing them from it. I often find myself tinkering with emerging technologies and researching human-machine interactions.

I've previously collaborated with scientists from IBM Quantum and the DLR Institute for AI Safety and Security to create interactive exhibits to represent quantum computing.

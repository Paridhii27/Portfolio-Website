This project began as a simple exploration of the word "echoes". At its core, the concept of echoes represents repetition, reverberation, and the persistence of sound or memory across time and space. It's not just the literal sound that bounces back, but the symbolic traces of moments, thoughts, and emotions that linger long after their origin.
